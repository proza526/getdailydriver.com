Frequently asked questions

Does Daily Driver sync across my devices?

Yes. If you're signed into iCloud, your tasks, notes, and projects sync automatically between your iPhone and iPad.

Is my data private?

Completely. Daily Driver stores all data on your device and in your private iCloud container. We don't have servers, accounts, or analytics. See our privacy policy for details.

Can I use Daily Driver offline?

Yes. The app works fully offline. Your data will sync when you reconnect to the internet.

How do I use the focus timer?

Tap the timer icon from the main screen or the overflow menu. Set your duration and start focusing. The timer appears on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island so you can track progress without opening the app.

How do I rearrange tiles on the home screen?

Tap the overflow menu (...) in the top right, then select "Edit Layout." You can show, hide, and reorder tiles by tapping the eye icon or dragging them.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email us at support@getdailydriver.com with a description of what happened and what you expected. Screenshots are always helpful.
