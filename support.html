FAQ

Things people ask.

Does Daily Driver sync across my devices?
Yes. If you're signed into iCloud on both devices, your tasks, notes, and projects sync automatically between your iPhone and iPad. Sync happens through your private iCloud container — we don't see any of it.
Is my data private?
Completely. Daily Driver has no server, no account system, and no analytics. Your data lives on your device and in your private iCloud container. See the privacy policy for the full story.
Does it work offline?
Yes. The app is fully offline-capable. Anything you do while offline will sync up the next time your device reconnects to iCloud.
How does the focus timer work?
Open the Focus tab, pick Focus / Short Break / Long Break, and tap play. Your session appears on the Lock Screen and (on supported devices) the Dynamic Island, so you can keep an eye on it without unlocking your phone.
How do I rearrange the tiles on my home screen?
From the Today screen, tap the overflow menu (•••) in the top-right and choose "Edit Layout." Then tap the eye icon next to a tile to show or hide it, or drag the handle to reorder.
Sync isn't working between my iPhone and iPad. What now?
First, confirm both devices are signed into the same iCloud account and have iCloud Drive enabled in Settings. Then open Daily Driver on both and pull-to-refresh on the Today screen. If you're still seeing patchy sync, email us at support@getdailydriver.com with your iOS version and what you're seeing.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Email support@getdailydriver.com with what you were doing, what you expected, and what actually happened. Screenshots or a screen recording help a lot.
I have an idea for a feature. Do you take requests?
Yes — please send them. Daily Driver is shaped a lot by the people using it. Even if a request doesn't make it in, you'll get a reply.
