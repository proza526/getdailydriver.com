Frequently asked questions

Is Daily Driver free?

Yes! Daily Driver is free to download and use. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but the core app will always be free.

Does Daily Driver sync across devices?

Yes. If you're signed into iCloud, your tasks, notes, and projects sync automatically between your iPhone and iPad.

Do you collect my data?

No. Daily Driver has no servers, no analytics, and no tracking. Your data stays on your devices and in your private iCloud container. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use Daily Driver on my Mac?

Not yet, but Mac support is on our roadmap. Stay tuned!

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual items in the app, or delete all data by removing the app from your device. iCloud data can be managed through Settings > Apple ID > iCloud.

The app isn't syncing. What should I do?

Make sure you're signed into the same iCloud account on all devices, that iCloud Drive is enabled, and that you have an internet connection. Try closing and reopening the app.
